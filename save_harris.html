Best Wishes, Harris Sankar!

Bro, I still owe you many.

Bro, eight years of friendship and I still owe you a lot—for all the times you put up with my nonsense. We’ve been friends since 8th standard, took different majors, but somehow always ended up in the same crazy situations.

Remember college Pongal when you couldn’t figure out how to wear a dhoti? Lucky for you, I was there to save the day before you turned it into a mess.

Our bike rides? Let’s just say we had more close calls than smooth rides. If there was a prize for “most near accidents,” we’d probably win—if we made it there.

Movie nights were mostly arguing about what to watch. I’d suggest a movie, you’d disagree, and we’d go back and forth. Still waiting for you to admit my picks are better, bro.

And Red Dead Redemption 2? We spent more time debating who’s the better character—Arthur Morgan or John Marston—than actually playing. If Rockstar ever needs opinions, they know who to call.

You’re not a vegetarian, but you stuck to veg-only places just because of me. Either you’re loyal or you just didn’t want to argue about food.

Also, I know you’re a fan of my photography, so expect some epic shots coming your way from Ghana—don’t say I didn’t warn you.

Cricket matches? Our skills weren’t great, but our commentary was on point.

Now you’re off to Ghana. Hopefully, you find fewer bike troubles, easier dhoti lessons, and some decent veg food. And bro, get ready—my movie suggestions and Red Dead opinions are coming with you.
